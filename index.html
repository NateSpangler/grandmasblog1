Our Story

When my husband was 12 years old, he was 6 feet tall and wore a size 12 shoe. For most of his teenage and adult years, he was confined to purchasing whatever was in the department store, and on the rack. My husband is now 6’5” and older, but still finds it difficult to find comfortable clothing and long sleeve shirts that fit his 7’ wingspan.

I named this site ‘Tried and True’ to help Big and Tall men find clothing that actually fits. Happy Shopping!
